Tenders

Notices issued by IMC

Tender For Road Construction

A tender for road construction invites contractors to submit bids for building or repairing roads. The process involves publishing a tender notice, providing detailed project specifications, and evaluating bids based on criteria such as cost, quality, and completion time to ensure the best value and compliance with standards.

Tender For Water Pipes

A tender for water pipes invites suppliers to submit bids to provide specified water pipes for a project. The process involves publishing a tender notice, detailed bid submission guidelines, and evaluating bids based on predefined criteria to ensure quality and cost-effectiveness.

Tender For Sewage Management

A tender for sewage management invites firms to submit bids for the construction, maintenance, or upgrade of sewage systems. The process includes detailed project requirements, submission guidelines, and evaluation criteria to ensure effective and sustainable sewage solutions.

Tender For websites

A tender for website development for the Indore Municipal Corporation invites bids from developers to design, build, and maintain the corporation's website. The process involves publishing a notice, outlining project requirements, and evaluating bids based on technical expertise, cost, and proposed functionality to ensure an effective online presence.

Questions

Nagar Nigam Indore is responsible for providing municipal services such as sanitation, water supply, street lighting, road maintenance, and waste management within the city limits.

The working hours are typically from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday to Saturday. Offices are closed on Sundays and public holidays.

The main office is located at Palika Plaza, M.G. Road, Indore, Madhya Pradesh.

You can contact them via their helpline number, email, or through the contact form on their official website.

Applications for trade licenses can be submitted online on the Nagar Nigam Indore website or at the municipal office.
